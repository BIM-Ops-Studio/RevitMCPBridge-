# RevitMCPBridge: The Documentary Series
## "Teaching AI to Build: A Journey from Concept to Construction Documents"

---

## Series Overview

**Total Runtime:** 15-18 minutes (6 episodes × 2.5-3 minutes each)
**Format:** Professional animated presentations with voice narration
**Audience:** AEC professionals, AI enthusiasts, software developers, investors
**Tone:** Inspiring, technical yet accessible, storytelling-driven

---

## The Narrative Arc

### Act 1: The Problem (Episodes 1-2)
*"Why does this matter?"*
- Architects waste 60-70% of time on repetitive documentation
- AI revolution happening everywhere except professional CAD/BIM
- The gap between natural language AI and Revit's complexity

### Act 2: The Solution (Episodes 3-4)
*"How we built it"*
- The MCP bridge architecture
- First breakthroughs and "it works!" moments
- Evolution from 30 methods to 449+ endpoints

### Act 3: The Future (Episodes 5-6)
*"Where this goes"*
- Autonomous workflow systems (Spine)
- The Level 5 vision: 90% automated CD sets
- What this means for the industry

## Visual Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep Blue (#1E3A5F) - Trust, technology
- **Accent:** Bright Orange (#FF6B35) - Energy, innovation
- **Secondary:** Light Gray (#F4F4F4) - Clean, professional
- **Highlight:** Cyan (#00D4FF) - AI, digital

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Montserrat Bold (modern, architectural)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (clean, readable)
- **Code/Tech:** JetBrains Mono (developer aesthetic)

### Animation Principles
- **Builds:** Left-to-right reveal (progress metaphor)
- **Transitions:** Smooth fade/slide (professional)
- **Data:** Animated counters, growing charts
- **Architecture diagrams:** Draw-on-screen effect

### Visual Motifs
- **The Bridge:** Recurring visual of connection forming
- **Growth Lines:** Charts showing trajectory
- **Code Snippets:** Clean, syntax-highlighted
- **Revit Screenshots:** Before/after comparisons
