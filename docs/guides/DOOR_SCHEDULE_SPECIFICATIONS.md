## DOOR TYPE SPECIFICATIONS

### TYPE-A: Door-Passage-Single-Flush (36" x 80") - 6 doors
**Usage:** Unit Entry Doors from Corridor
**Size:** 36" x 80" (nominal) / 34" clear width
**Fire Rating:** 20-minute (FBC Table 716.5 - Corridor Opening)
**Frame Type:** Hollow Metal Frame
**Frame Material:** 16-gauge steel
**Frame Finish:** Paint finish to match
**Door Material:** Solid core wood or hollow metal
**Hardware Set:** HW-01
- Lever handle (ADA compliant) - ANSI A156.2, Grade 1
- Heavy duty hinges (3-1/2" x 3-1/2") - ANSI A156.1
- Door closer - ANSI A156.4 (Fire rated, ADA compliant <5 lbf)
- Peephole 43"-47" AFF (160° wide angle)
- Kick plate (10" high, stainless steel)
- Silencers (3 per door)
**Self-Closing:** Yes (required for fire-rated corridor doors)
**Undercut:** 1/2" for sound rating
**Code Notes:**
- 20-minute rating required per FBC Table 716.5
- ADA hardware required (Type A units)
- Positive latching required (FBC 716.2.6.4)

## EXIT STAIR DOORS - CRITICAL

**Identification Needed:** Which doors serve the exit stairs?
**Likely candidates:** 36" x 80" or 42" x 80" doors marked as exit doors

**Exit Stair Door Requirements:**
**Fire Rating:** 90-minute (1-1/2 hour) per FBC Table 716.5 (2-hour stair enclosure)
**Frame Type:** Hollow Metal Frame (fire-rated)
**Frame Material:** 16-gauge steel minimum
**Door Material:** Solid core wood (20-minute rated) or hollow metal
**Hardware Set:** HW-06 (Exit Hardware)
- Panic hardware - ANSI A156.3 (Fire rated, ADA compliant)
- Heavy duty hinges - ANSI A156.1
- Door closer - ANSI A156.4 (Fire rated)
- Coordinator (if pairs)
- Silencers
**Self-Closing:** Yes (required)
**Self-Latching:** Yes (positive latching required)
**Swing:** Direction of egress
**Width:** 36" nominal minimum (32" clear)
**Code Notes:**
- FBC 1010.1.2: Exit doors swing in direction of egress
- FBC 1010.1.9: Panic hardware if occupant load >50
- FBC Table 716.5: 90-minute door in 2-hour enclosure
- Must be labeled with fire rating

## ACCESSIBILITY REQUIREMENTS (All 6 Units are Type A)

**All doors in Type A units must have:**
- Lever-type hardware (no knobs)
- Maximum 5 lbf operating force
- Hardware mounted 34"-48" AFF
- Maneuvering clearances per ADA Fig. 404.2.4.1:
  - Pull side: 18" (latch side)
  - Push side: 12" (latch side) if door has closer
  - Clear width: 32" minimum when open 90°

**Minimum Door Widths (Type A):**
- Unit entry from corridor: 36" nominal (32" clear) ✓
- Bathroom doors: 32" nominal (30" clear) ⚠️ CHECK TYPE-F
- Bedroom doors: 30" nominal minimum
- All other interior: 32" nominal preferred

---

## FIRE-RATED DOOR REQUIREMENTS

**20-Minute Doors (Corridor Openings):**
- Label required (manufacturer's label on door edge)
- Self-closing device required
- Positive latching required
- Maximum 1/8" clearance (door to frame)
- Gasketing (smoke seal) if required
- No louvers or grilles

**90-Minute Doors (Exit Stair):**
- Label required (1-1/2 hour or 90-minute)
- Self-closing and self-latching required
- Vision panels maximum 100 sq in (if provided)
- Maximum 1/8" clearance
- NO GLASS unless fire-rated and labeled

## IMPACT-RESISTANT REQUIREMENTS
