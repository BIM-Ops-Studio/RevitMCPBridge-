## Glass Types

### Glass Makeup
| Type | Description | Use |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Monolithic | Single pane | Interior, mirrors |
| Insulated (IGU) | 2+ panes with air space | Standard exterior |
| Laminated | 2 panes with interlayer | Safety, security |
| Tempered | Heat-treated for strength | Safety, wind loads |
| Heat-strengthened | Partially tempered | Thermal stress |

### IGU Construction
```
Typical makeup:
[Outdoor] Glass / Air Space / Glass [Indoor]
         1/4"     1/2"      1/4"     = 1" overall

High-performance:
[Outdoor] Glass / Argon / Glass [Indoor]
         1/4"    1/2"    1/4"    + Low-E coating
```

### Coatings
| Type | Location | Purpose |
|------|----------|---------|
| Low-E (hard coat) | #2 or #3 | Solar control |
| Low-E (soft coat) | #2 | Energy efficiency |
| Reflective | #1 or #2 | Glare, solar control |
| Tinted | In glass | Solar control, aesthetics |

### Surface Numbering
```
#1 = Exterior surface of outer pane
#2 = Interior surface of outer pane
#3 = Exterior surface of inner pane
#4 = Interior surface of inner pane
```

## Safety Glazing Requirements

### Locations Requiring Safety Glazing (IBC 2406)
- Doors and sidelites (within 24" of door)
- Shower and tub enclosures
- Railings and guards
- Within 36" of walking surface and 60" of bottom edge
- Sloped glazing (skylights)
- At pools and hot tubs (within 60")

### Safety Glass Types
| Type | Impact Resistance | Breakage Pattern |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| Tempered | 4× stronger than annealed | Small cubes |
| Laminated | Holds when broken | Cracks but stays in place |
| Wired | Limited | Holds in frame |

| Zone | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| HVHZ (Miami-Dade) | Large missile (9 lb 2×4 @ 50 fps) |

|------|-------------|

| Laminated (0.060" PVB × 2) | Enhanced impact |

## Thermal Bridge Mitigation

### Problem Areas
- Frame at slab edge
- Mullion at spandrel
- Sill at interior
- Around openings

### Solutions
| Solution | Location |
|----------|----------|
| Thermal break frame | In mullion profile |
| Insulated spandrel | Behind opaque panels |
| Thermal isolator | At slab edge |
| Warm edge spacer | IGU perimeter |

## Specification Considerations

### Performance Specs to Include
- Design pressure (DP rating)

- Acoustic rating (if required)

### Submittal Requirements
- Shop drawings with setting blocks
- Glass samples and calculations
- Structural calculations
- Mock-up (large projects)
- Warranty documentation

### Installation Requirements
- Setting blocks at quarter points
- Edge clearance per manufacturer
- Sealant compatibility
- Drainage provisions
- Protection during construction
