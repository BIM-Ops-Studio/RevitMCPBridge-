## Fire/Life Safety

### Occupancy
| Use | Classification |
|-----|----------------|
| Guest rooms | R-1 |
| Assembly (restaurant) | A-2 |
| Assembly (ballroom) | A-3 |
| Business office | B |

### Separation Requirements
| Separation | Rating |
|------------|--------|
| Guest room to corridor | 1 hour |
| Guest room to guest room | 1 hour |
| Assembly to other | 2 hour |
| Stair enclosure | 2 hour (high-rise) |

### High-Rise Requirements (75'+)
- Automatic sprinklers throughout
- Smoke detection in rooms
- Stair pressurization
- Fire service elevator
- Emergency voice/alarm
- Standpipe system
